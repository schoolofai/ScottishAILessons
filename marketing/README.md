## üìä Reporting & Tracking

### Weekly Review (15 minutes)
1. **Google Search Console**:
   - Check Coverage: Any new pages indexed?
   - Review Performance: Impressions, clicks, CTR
   - Identify crawl errors

2. **Google Analytics 4**:
   - Organic traffic trend
   - Top landing pages
   - Conversion rate

3. **Keyword Rankings** (Ahrefs/Semrush):
   - Track top 20 target keywords
   - Note position changes

### Monthly Deep Dive (2 hours)
1. **Content Audit**: Which pages need updates?
2. **Backlink Analysis**: New backlinks acquired?
3. **Technical Health Check**: PageSpeed, broken links, GSC errors
4. **Competitor Analysis**: What are competitors doing?
5. **Strategy Adjustment**: What's working? What needs change?
